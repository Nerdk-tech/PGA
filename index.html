Program Guidelines

Eligibility & Requirements

General Eligibility

  • U.S. citizen or permanent resident
  • 18 years of age or older
  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Active U.S. bank account
  • No active federal financial delinquency

Business Requirements

  • Business legally registered in the U.S.
  • Comprehensive business plan required
  • Last 3 months of bank statements
  • EIN (Employer Identification Number)
  • Proof of business operations

FAQ

  • Processing: 2–4 weeks (some instant)
  • Apply for multiple grants at once
  • Funds disbursed to your bank account
  • Re-apply after 90 days if denied
  • All applications reviewed by officers

Important Notice

  • Platinum Grant Access is a private platform
  • We facilitate — not disburse — grants
  • All info must be accurate and truthful
  • Fraudulent applications reported to authorities
  • Grant funds must be used as stated

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal identification (name, date of birth, address)
  • Contact information (email, phone number)
  • Financial information (bank account details)
  • Government-issued identification documents
  • Business information and documentation

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and facilitate grant applications
  • Verify your identity and eligibility
  • Communicate about application status
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption. All data is stored securely and access is strictly controlled. We comply with SOC 2 Type II security standards.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share with grant funding agencies as necessary, service providers, and law enforcement when required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary for the purposes collected, and as required by law. Grant records are retained for a minimum of 7 years.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact: support@platinumgrantaccess.com

7. Contact

Privacy questions: support@platinumgrantaccess.com
